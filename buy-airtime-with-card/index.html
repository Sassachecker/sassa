How to Buy Airtime With a SASSA Card on My Phone?

  • Post published:April 16, 2024

Ever found yourself short of airtime and only had your SASSA card on you? You’re in luck! With just a few clicks on your phone, you can buy airtime using your SASSA card. It’s a simple, secure, and convenient process.

This guide will walk you through the steps of registering for mobile banking services, navigating the USSD menu, and finally, purchasing airtime with your SASSA card. While you’re at it, ensure you conduct a SASSA status check to stay updated on any changes or updates related to your card.

How to Buy Airtime with a SASSA Card on My Phone | Registering Mobile Banking Services

Before you can buy airtime with your SASSA card on your phone, you’ll need to register for mobile banking services.

  1. You’ll need to visit your bank’s website or download their mobile banking app. Most banks provide these services for free. But make sure the app or bank has the option to do transactions with the SASSA Card.
  2. Once you’ve accessed your bank’s mobile banking portal, look for the ‘Register’ or ‘Sign up’ option.
  3. Here, you’ll be asked to put the information, including full name, date of birth, and Social Security Number.
  4. You’ll also be asked to create a username and password.
  5. After completing the account creation, you’ll usually need to verify your account.
  6. This is often done through a verification email or text message sent to the cell number that you provided.
  7. Once verified, you’re ready to use mobile banking!
  8. With mobile banking, you can manage your account, make transactions, and even purchase airtime with your SASSA card, all from the comfort of your phone.

Navigating the USSD Menu

How do you navigate the USSD menu to purchase airtime with your SASSA card on your phone?

  1. Firstly, dial the USSD code provided by your bank or mobile service provider.
  2. This might be the format *XXX#.
  3. Press the call or send button, and you’ll be taken to the USSD menu.
  4. The USSD menu is essentially a list of options. Each option corresponds to a different function, such as ‘check balance’, ‘transfer funds’, or ‘buy airtime’.
  5. These options are typically numbered, so to select an option, you simply need to reply with the number of the option you want to choose.
  6. To buy airtime, look for an option that says ‘buy airtime’ or something similar. Once you’ve found it, reply with the corresponding number.
  7. Type in your desired amount and press send.
  8. Finally, enter your SASSA card PIN to complete the transaction.
  9. This is a security measure to ensure it’s you making the purchase. Enter your PIN and press send. The confirmation message will be sent to you after this.

Purchasing Airtime With SASSA Card

  1. First, ensure that you have sufficient funds in your SASSA account. If there’s not enough balance, you’ll need to deposit funds before you can buy airtime. Once you’ve ensured you have sufficient funds, dial 1306787# on your phone. This is the USSD code for the SASSA menu.
  2. Once you’ve dialed the code, a menu will appear on your screen. Choose the option ‘Prepaid’. This option will lead you to another menu where you can select ‘Airtime’.
  3. After selecting ‘Airtime’, you’ll be prompted to enter the amount you want to buy for airtime. Be careful here, make sure you input the correct amount.
  4. After you’ve entered the amount, you’ll be asked to confirm your purchase. If everything looks good, select ‘Yes’ to confirm.
  5. Additionally, you should receive a text message confirming your purchase.

Remember, the funds will be deducted directly from your SASSA account. So, before you make any purchase, always ensure that you have enough balance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Use My SASSA Card to Purchase Airtime for Someone Else?

Yes, you can use your SASSA card to buy airtime for someone else. Just follow the normal airtime purchase process, but enter the other person’s number when prompted.

What Other Services Can I Access Using Mobile Banking With My SASSA Card?

With your SASSA card, you can access numerous mobile banking services. You’re able to check your balance, transfer funds, pay bills, and even purchase electricity. It’s a convenient way to manage your funds remotely.

Can I Purchase Data Bundles With My SASSA Card as Well?

Yes, you can buy data bundles with your SASSA card too. The process is similar to buying airtime.

Is It Possible to Reverse an Airtime Purchase Made With a SASSA Card?

Yes, it’s possible to reverse an airtime purchase made with a SASSA card. However, you’ll need to contact your service provider immediately and follow their procedures for reversing transactions. It’s not guaranteed, but worth trying.

Conclusion

So, you’ve got it! Now you know how to buy airtime using your SASSA card on your phone. It’s as simple as registering for mobile banking, navigating the USSD menu, and making the purchase. It’s all about convenience at your fingertips. Don’t hesitate to utilize this feature to stay connected. Happy airtime buying!
