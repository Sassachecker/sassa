SASSA Disability Grant Payment Date and Status for September 2021

  • Post published:April 16, 2024

You’re keen to know the old dates of funds, and we’re here to provide that information about the SASSA Disability Grant Payment Date and Status for September 2021. However, you should know that SASSA’s grant payments have been postponed for 2024.

SASSA has offered various grants with each of its eligibility criteria. The most popular is the SRD grant of R350, but if you’ve applied for the Disability Grant then you need to check the status of your grant whether it is accepted, declined, or pending. You can check the status on our official Website https://sassachecker.co.za/.

However, other grants cater to specific needs, including disability, old age, and child support, all these grants are released on the previous dates mentioned on our blog.

SASSA Disability Grant Payment Date and Status for September 2021

You’ll be delighted to know that SASSA schedules its payments to ensure beneficiaries receive their grants on time. For September 2021, expect the Disability grant to be disbursed on 06/09/2021.

SASSA has paid the grant for September 2021. If you’re willing to check the status of the other grant payments, you can visit our homepage.

In case of changes, SASSA will provide timely updates.

How to Apply for Sassa Disability Grant?

First, you or your child must be eligible for the grant. If yes, then the process is straightforward and can be applied online.

  • Must be a South African citizen, asylum or refugee
  • Insufficient income (income below R350/month)
  • No other UIF grant payments
  • Not having any other financial support

To apply for the SASSA Grant Payment, follow the below steps.

1. Head over to the Sassa official website (services.sassa.gov.za) and create a profile if you don’t have one yet.

2. This will enable you to access various Sassa services, including grant applications.

3. Fill in your phone number which you’re using currently should be valid. Verify your phone number after getting an SMS containing OTP.

4. Once logged in, navigate to the “Apply for a Grant” section and choose “Disability Support Grant.” Fill out the application form carefully, ensuring you provide all the required information.

5. Enter all the details including your South African ID or valid refugee/asylum seeker document, this must be a bar-coded document.

6. Proof of residence, proof of income, and banking details.

7. After carefully filling in all the details simply hit the submit button and wait for its approval.

Status of Your Grant

The status of your Sassa Grant might be something that you are curious about. Is it pending, declined, or failed? Here are the details.

Payment Date for Sassa SRD R350 Grant?

Typically, the payment is released on 1, 2, or 10 dates of the month. However, delays can occur due to administrative reasons or public holidays. Check the SASSA website regularly for updates on the exact payment date.

Regarding the status of the grant, it’s usually approved if you meet the eligibility criteria.

Sassa Grant Status is Pending?

If your SASSA grant status shows as ‘pending’, it’s often a sign that your application is still under review. This means the authorities are still processing your documents and haven’t made a decision yet.

It’s essential to keep checking the status regularly for updates. If you see your status pending for a long period then approach the Sassa. They’ll provide the necessary guidance to resolve any possible issues.

Sassa Grant status Is Declined?

Seeing your SASSA grant status as ‘declined’ can be disheartening.

A declined status typically indicates that your application doesn’t meet the eligibility criteria. You might’ve failed to provide the necessary documentation, or perhaps the income of your household exceeds the grant’s threshold.

Don’t despair, though. You have the right to request an explanation from SASSA within 90 days of the decision. Keep all your correspondence and documents as proof.

Sassa Grant Status is Failed?

This status merely indicates that there’s an issue that’s preventing the grant from being processed. It could be a missing document, incorrect information, or a system error.

Your first step should be to contact SASSA immediately for clarification and guidance. They’ll help you understand what went wrong and how to rectify it. Once the problem is resolved, your application will be reprocessed.

Conclusion

In conclusion, you’ve got the SASSA Disability Grant Payment Date and Status for September 2021. and status can be also checked for future payments.  If there are any additional inquiries or uncertainties, please don’t hesitate to contact us either by commenting below or contacting us directly. Our team is working to answer all of your queries.
