SASSA Offices Near Me 

  • Post published:April 16, 2024


SASSA, the South African Social Security Agency, is your one-stop destination for social grants and assistance. We’ve simplified your search by putting together a list of SASSA offices in various South African cities. No need to spend hours searching – we’ve done the work for you. Just find your nearest city and head to the respective office. With our ‘SASSA Offices Near Me’ guide, help is always within reach.

SASSA Offices Near Me 

You’ll find several SASSA offices conveniently located across South Africa, ready to provide you with essential social security services and manual Sassa status check services. Whether you’re in bustling Johannesburg or relaxed Cape Town, there’s a SASSA office nearby ready to assist you.

If you’re thinking about applying for a grant, it’s crucial to visit the nearest SASSA office. The experienced personnel there will assist you in navigating the application process, making sure you grasp all the necessary criteria and deadlines. Additionally, they will provide support in updating your information and resolving any issues that may arise during the grant application process.

Finding Your Nearest SASSA Office

In search of your nearest SASSA office? Don’t stress. It’s easier than you might think to find the closest location to you.

The first step is to identify the city closest to you. Whether you’re in Cape Town, Durban, or somewhere else, there’s likely a SASSA office nearby. Once you’ve pinpointed your city, it’s time to narrow down the list from below.

Alternatively, you can utilize Google Maps. Type in ‘SASSA office’ followed by your city name, and you’ll see a map pinpointing the location of the office.

Operating Hours of SASSA Offices

Generally, SASSA offices are open from Monday to Friday, starting from 7:30 am to 4:00 pm. Remember, SASSA offices are usually busiest at the beginning and end of the month, so try to plan your visit accordingly.

Also, while SASSA strives to serve all clients within their working hours, try to be there early. Arriving late might result in you not being attended to, especially on busy days.

Contact Information for SASSA Offices

  • You can contact SASSA’s national call center at 0800 60 10 11. It’s toll-free when you dial from a landline.
  • They’re open on weekdays from 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. and can answer your queries or direct you to the right office.
  • Besides, you can email SASSA at GrantEnquiries@sassa.gov.za for any grant-related questions or issues.
