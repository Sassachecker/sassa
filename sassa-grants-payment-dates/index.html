SASSA Grants Payment Dates 2023: Excellent Guide

  • Post published:February 25, 2023

SASSA is an official, well-known, and prominent social welfare organization. It provides social and financial help to the old, and needy people including disabled and deserving students. All the deserving are eagerly waiting for the SASSA grants payment dates in 2023.

The payment and pentons help the people to lift their standards of living and not go to the complex side. The unguarded people feel so safe with the amount under the official name of the government of South Africa. The institute is growing so well with its mission.

What are the SASSA Grants Payment Dates 2023?

For the past 2 decades, SASSA has been giving financial availability to those who could not earn a living or for those who could not fulfill all their needs with their earnings. This institute of government releases payments every month.

The SASSA grants payment dates for 2023 were announced as soon as the year started. Following are the complete details about the release date for each category.

Sassa Grants Payments For January

  • The older people get their grants or you can say the payment by the 3rd of Jan, 2023.
  • The disabled will grant their payments by the 4th of Jan, 2023.
  • Lastly, the needy children will get their payments by the 5th of Jan, 2023.

Sassa Grants Payments For February

  • The older people get their grants by the 2nd of Feb, 2023.
  • The disabled will grant their payments by the 3rd of Feb, 2023.
  • Lastly, the needy children will get their payments by the 6th of Feb, 2023.

 Sassa Grants Payments For March

  • The older people get their grants by the 2nd of March, 2023.
  • The disabled will grant their payments by the 3rd of March, 2023.
  • Lastly, the needy children will get their payments by the 6th of March, 2023.

Sassa Grant Payments For April

  • The older people get their grants by the 2nd of April, 2023.
  • The Disabled persons will grant their payments by the 3rd of April, 2023
  • Foster care will be released by April 8, 2023
  • Lastly, the needy children will get their payments by the 8th of April, 2023.

Sassa Grant Payments For June

  • The Elderly Person Grant will be paid out on June 2, 2023,
  • The Disabled Persons Grant will be disbursed on June 3, 2023.
  • Other grant funds are scheduled to be paid on June 6, 2023.

Sassa Grant Payments For July

  • Elderly Person Grant: July 2, 2023
  • Disabled Person Grant: July 3, 2023
  • Other grant funds: July 6, 2023

When will the Recipients Withdraw the amount?

Since many recipients can encounter challenges on the paid date, SASSA recommends all recipients avoid withdrawing the amount on the same release date. However, the money is theirs, so they can withdraw the money after any date other than the release date.

Various Methods to Collect SASSA Grant

All recipients are urged by SASSA to use various methods for gaining access to their funds. Retailers and ATMs are two of the techniques.

The SASSA Gold card is still valid for usage by social grant recipients, and they are able to use it anywhere that accepts bank cards. The card may also be used at establishments across the country that offer the cashback feature.

The various SASSA SRD grant payment methods are:

  • Bank ATM
  • Boxer
  • Pick n pay
  • Checkers
  • U-Save
  • Shoprite
